MSE: 64.66 Model Score: 0.07

The data from the linear regression was split into training and test datasets in order to conduct linear machine learning using sklearn in python. The model was only able to successfully predict percentage of women managers based on percentage of women in the workforce 7% of the time.

The data suggest that the number of women in the workforce does not relate to the number of women who are managers. This implies that women are not entering managerial roles proportionate to their participation in the labor force. That is, as more women enter the labor force, more women are not becoming managers.
